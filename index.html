What is Programming Party?

Programming Party is a community of passionate builders at Pacific Lutheran University. Every week, we gather to work on creative, unconventional software projects. Over 7 years, we've built everything from music-based games to energy management simulators to emotion-detecting webcam games.

Our Mission: Celebrate collaborative creativity, learn together, and have fun building things that don't have to be practical to be amazing.
