About Programming Party

Our Story

Programming Party started in 2019 as a way for PLU students to collaborate on creative software projects. What began as a small group has grown into a vibrant community of builders, thinkers, and creatives.

What We Build

Our projects range from serious applications to delightfully silly experiments. We believe that the best learning happens when you're having fun, so we embrace unconventional ideas and playful execution.

Community Values

  • Creativity First: We value creative thinking over production-grade code
  • Learning Over Winning: Failed experiments teach as much as successful projects
  • Collaboration: We build together, not alone
  • Fun: If it's not fun, why are we doing it?
  • Openness: All skill levels welcome

Join Us

Programming Party meets weekly during the school year. Whether you're a seasoned developer or just learning to code, there's a place for you. Email Dr. Caley at caleyjb@plu.edu to get involved, or check out our GitHub organization to see our projects.
